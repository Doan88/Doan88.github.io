About Me

My name is Thanh Truong Doan. I am currently in my 5th semester of the Software Development program at Mohawk College.

During my time there, I gained extensive technical experience and also spent four years as a Senior Technician, leading projects involving hardware and software installation, configuration, and major system upgrades.

In 2023, I decided to return to school to pursue a new career path in Software Development—a journey that has brought both challenges and excitement. Over the past four semesters, I have acquired new knowledge and hands-on skills in a completely new field while actively seeking opportunities to apply them in real-world environments.

As part of my practical experience, I completed a Co-op IT System Assistant placement at the Sopinka Courthouse in Hamilton. In this role, I performed daily equipment and JVN (Justice Network System) checks to ensure smooth and uninterrupted court operations, assisted judges and staff in troubleshooting both software (Outlook, Office 365, Court Assist Software, etc.) and hardware (desktop, laptop, printer, sound system, etc.) issues, and logged IT tickets while coordinating with vendors to efficiently resolve technical problems. This experience strengthened my technical troubleshooting, problem-solving, and professional communication skills in a real-world IT environment.

I am particularly interested in full-stack development, as I enjoy understanding both how the front end and back end work together to create complete, functional applications. I appreciate how the back end handles core logic while the front end delivers user experience and immediate feedback. However, as a new developer, I am open to any role that helps me strengthen my skills, expand my experience, and grow professionally. My long-term goal is to build software that can positively impact people's daily lives across all ages.
